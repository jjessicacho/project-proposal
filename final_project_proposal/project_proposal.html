Client Information

  • Client: Oh! Zzhusam Korean Restaurant
  • Business Type: Family style Korean restaurant located in Burnaby BC Canada
  • Problems:
    • The 3rd party of delivery companies/partners such as UberEats, DoorDash, SkipTheDish take 25-30% of the store's profits from delivery orders.
    • Those delivery platforms are also too complicated to manage, especially during busy hours. This resulted customers do not receive any notifications when their order is cancelled.
    • With social network platforms, it is difficult to showcase and update their menu on online in a regular base.
    • Many customers are often confused about the price and the portion size due to lack of understanding Korean dining culture.
    • Although their business has been going for years, there are still few customers having hard time finding the store's location and some available parking lot spaces near to the store.
  • Special Requests:
    • Keep their original logo and graphic design

Prime Objectives

  • Theme: Promoting my friend's Korean restaurant business in British Columbia
  • Objectives:
    1. Create a fully responsive website focused on simple online ordering system.
    2. Create an organized menu with high quality Korean dish photos and lots of iconography indicating spicy levels and portion sizes.
    3. Display clear view of its location using high quality images and Google map platforms.

Ideal Audience

  • Name: John Kim
  • Age: 27
  • Gender: Male
  • Location: Lives in the Lower Mainland in British Columbia
  • Language: Bilingual (English and Korean)
  • Budget: $50-80 (including 10% of tips)
  • Bio: Lives with his roommates, K-food lover, works in the sushi restaurant. After a long day at work, he likes to try out new Korean foods in Vancouver with his friends and family.
  • Wants and Needs
    • Looking for a cozy Korean restaurant near to his house for a family dinner
    • Prefers ordering various main dishes and sharing those with others
    • Not only his preferences, he needs to find a place where his family can eat too.
    • His dad loves drinking Soju.
    • His mom loves eating spicy sea foods.
    • His little sister want to eat chicken.

    • Image-box02: Main Dishes
    • Image-box03: Sides

    • Page01: Appetizers

    • Page03: Sides
